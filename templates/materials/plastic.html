How to Recycle Plastic Waste

  • Rinse out all containers before recycling them.
  • Flatten cardboard boxes and paper products to save space in recycling bins.
  • Separate plastic bags from other recyclables.
  • Check with your local recycling program to see what types of plastic they accept.

Benefits of Recycling Plastic Waste

  • Reduces the amount of plastic waste that goes to landfills.
  • Conserves natural resources.
  • Reduces greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Creates jobs in the recycling industry.
